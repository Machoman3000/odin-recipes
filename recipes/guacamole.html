Guacamole

Description

This authentic guacamole recipe is fresh, zesty, and incredibly easy to make. Creamy avocados are combined with lime juice, cilantro, and just the right amount of heat from jalape√±o peppers.

Perfect as a dip with tortilla chips, a topping for tacos, or a spread for your favorite Mexican dishes. Best served immediately while the avocados are bright green and fresh!

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Cut avocados in half and remove the pit. Scoop out the flesh into a mixing bowl.
  2. Mash the avocado with a fork until it reaches your desired consistency (chunky or smooth).
  3. Add lime juice and salt. Mix well.
  4. Fold in the onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic.
  5. Add cayenne pepper to taste.
  6. Refrigerate for 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.
