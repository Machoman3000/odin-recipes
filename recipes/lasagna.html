Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort food featuring layers of wide pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. This hearty dish has been a family favorite for generations.

Perfect for Sunday dinners or special occasions, lasagna is best served hot from the oven with a side of garlic bread and a fresh green salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 28 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 6 oz tomato paste
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 eggs
  • Fresh basil and parsley
  • Salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Brown the ground beef and sausage in a large skillet. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add onion and garlic to the meat. Cook until onion is soft.
  5. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and seasonings. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, eggs, half the Parmesan, and fresh herbs.
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish.
  8. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  9. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake 25 minutes more until bubbly and golden.
  11. Let rest 10-15 minutes before serving.
